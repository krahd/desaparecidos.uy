During Uruguay's latest dictatorship (1973-1985), 294 political activist where "disappeared" (desaparecidos, in Spanish). This vanishing of people has not permitted their families to achieve closure, and until today, their families are searching for their remains.
I would like to create a website that would implement a crawler that would follow every single linked page in the Internet, searching for faces that have a feature resembling one feature of one of the "desaparecidos", emulating the search of these families. The website will constantly roam the internet, re-creating the faces of the disappeared with the found features, and keeping a browsable database of the websites where the faces with the similar features are found. The artwork, will be shown on a dedicated url (desaparecidos.uy, or similar) and will show in real time the process of the webcrawler.
